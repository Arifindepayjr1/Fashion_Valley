About this product

The 2024 version of the Air Jordan 3 Retro OG 'Black Cement' stays true to the 1988 original, rolling out a black tumbled leather upper with grey elephant print overlays on the forefoot and heel. An embroidered Jumpman starts on the tongue, while a raised Nike Air logo decorates the heel. A white foam midsole equips Air sole cushioning, adding a touch of black along the heel, anchored by a grey rubber outsole.
